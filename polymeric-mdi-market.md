<p><p>The Polymeric MDI market is categorized primarily into two types: Brown Liquid and Light Brown Liquid. Brown Liquid MDI typically exhibits higher viscosity and is used in applications requiring strong adhesion and flexibility, such as insulation and coatings. Conversely, Light Brown Liquid MDI has lower viscosity, making it easier to mix and process, often utilized in manufacturing foams and sealants. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and furniture manufacturing, driving demand for tailored chemical solutions.</p></p>
