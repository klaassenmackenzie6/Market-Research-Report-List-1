<p><ul><li>Stereo Vision</li><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time-of-Flight</li><li>Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D electro-optical image sensor market includes technologies like **Stereo Vision**, which uses dual cameras to capture depth by comparing images. **Structured Light** projects patterns onto objects, measuring distortions to calculate depth. **Time-of-Flight** sensors emit light pulses, measuring their return time to determine distance. **Ultrasound** uses sound waves to map objects by analyzing echo reflections. Each method offers unique advantages in accuracy, speed, and application suitability, catering to industries like robotics, automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics for depth perception and object detection.</p></p>
