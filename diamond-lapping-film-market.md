<p><p>Diamond lapping films are categorized based on their backing materials, primarily into PSA (Pressure Sensitive Adhesive) backing, plain backing, and other types. PSA backing films offer convenient application by adhering directly to surfaces, making them user-friendly for various polishing tasks. Plain backing films, on the other hand, require additional adhesive for mounting, providing flexibility in usage. Other market types may include specialized films designed for unique applications or varying grit sizes, catering to diverse industrial and manufacturing needs for precision finishing.</p></p>
