<p><p>Slip masterbatch is a concentrated additive used in plastic manufacturing to improve the surface properties of polymer products. It helps to enhance the slip characteristics of films and molded parts, reducing friction, improving processing, and facilitating easier handling. Typically composed of additives like glycerol monostearate or erucamide, slip masterbatch is widely utilized in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.</p><p>The Slip Masterbatch Market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
