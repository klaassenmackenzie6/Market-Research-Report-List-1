<p><p>Residential epoxy floor coatings are durable, protective finishes applied to concrete floors. **Water-based epoxy** is eco-friendly, low-odor, and easy to clean, ideal for indoor use. **Solvent-based epoxy** offers superior durability and moisture resistance, suitable for high-traffic areas but emits strong fumes. **100% solids epoxy** contains no solvents or water, providing the highest durability and chemical resistance, perfect for heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and ease of application for residential spaces.</p></p>
