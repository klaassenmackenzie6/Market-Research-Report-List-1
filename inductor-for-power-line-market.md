<p><ul><li>Winding</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Film Sheet</li><li>Braided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductors in the power line market come in various types that cater to different applications. Winding inductors utilize coiled wire for efficient magnetic field generation. Laminated inductors combine thin layers of magnetic material to reduce energy loss and enhance performance. Film sheet inductors feature a flat construction, allowing for compact designs and high-frequency applications. Braided inductors utilize interwoven wires to optimize flexibility and reduce electromagnetic interference, making them suitable for environments requiring improved signal integrity. Each type serves distinct roles in power management and signal processing.</p></p>
