<p><p>The Precision Guidance Kit (PGK) enhances the accuracy of artillery munitions through advanced guidance systems. The market comprises two main types: GPS Guidance Kits and Integrated GPS/INS Guidance Kits. GPS Guidance Kits utilize satellite positioning to improve strike precision, while Integrated GPS/INS Kits combine Global Positioning System data with Inertial Navigation System inputs to enhance resilience against jamming and maintain accuracy in diverse environments. Both types are crucial for modern military operations, ensuring effective and efficient targeting.</p></p>
