<p><p>The automotive tail light market primarily features two types: Halogen Lamps and LED Lamps. Halogen lamps are traditional, cost-effective, and widely used, offering a warm, yellowish light but with higher energy consumption and shorter lifespans. LED lamps, on the other hand, are modern, energy-efficient, and durable, providing brighter, sharper illumination with lower power usage and longer life. LED tail lights are increasingly popular due to their advanced technology, sleek design, and superior performance, though they are more expensive than halogen alternatives.</p></p>
