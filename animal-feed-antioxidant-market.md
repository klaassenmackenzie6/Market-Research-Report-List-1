<p><p>The animal feed antioxidant market is divided into two main types: natural antioxidants and synthetic antioxidants. Natural antioxidants, derived from plant sources like tocopherols, rosemary extract, and ascorbic acid, are favored for their health benefits and safety. In contrast, synthetic antioxidants, such as BHT and BHA, are man-made and used for their cost-effectiveness and prolonged shelf life. Both types serve to enhance the nutritional quality of animal feed by preventing oxidative spoilage and improving overall animal health.</p></p>
