<p><p>The agricultural colorants market primarily comprises two types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are water-soluble colorants used for coloring materials like seeds, fertilizers, and animal feeds, offering vibrant hues and ensuring uniform application. They are often more readily absorbed by surfaces. In contrast, pigments are insoluble colorants that provide long-lasting color and stability, ideal for creating coatings, plastics, and more durable applications. Both play critical roles in enhancing aesthetics, promoting brand recognition, and improving visibility in agricultural products.</p></p>
