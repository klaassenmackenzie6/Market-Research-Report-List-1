<p><p>The high-end premium tequila market is categorized into several types. Blanco tequila is unaged, showcasing the pure flavor of the agave. Reposado is aged in barrels for a minimum of two months, adding complexity and smoothness. Joven, or "young" tequila, blends unaged and aged spirits, offering a balanced profile. Añejo is aged for one to three years, resulting in rich, deeper flavors. Other market offerings may include extra añejo and flavored tequilas, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
