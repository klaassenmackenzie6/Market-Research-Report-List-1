<p><ul><li>Button Type</li><li>Induction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Door handle sensors are advanced security and convenience devices. **Button Type** sensors require physical interaction, such as pressing a button to unlock or lock doors, offering a tactile and straightforward user experience. **Induction Type** sensors, on the other hand, use proximity detection, automatically unlocking doors when a user's hand approaches, providing a touchless and hygienic solution. Both types cater to modern security needs, with Button Type emphasizing simplicity and Induction Type focusing on seamless, contactless access, appealing to diverse market preferences.</p></p>
