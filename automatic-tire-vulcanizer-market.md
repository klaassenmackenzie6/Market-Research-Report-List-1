<p><ul><li>Mechanical vulcanizer</li><li>Hydraulic vulcanizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Tire Vulcanizer market is primarily segmented into mechanical and hydraulic vulcanizers. Mechanical vulcanizers utilize mechanical pressure for tire curing, suitable for smaller-scale operations with lower energy consumption. In contrast, hydraulic vulcanizers employ hydraulic pressure, offering higher efficiency and better uniformity in curing, making them ideal for large-scale production. Both types cater to different industry requirements, with mechanical vulcanizers often preferred for their simplicity, while hydraulic options are favored for their advanced performance and automation capabilities.</p></p>
