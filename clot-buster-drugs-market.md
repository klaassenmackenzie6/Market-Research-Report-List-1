<p><p>Clot buster drugs, or thrombolytics, are essential in treating conditions like heart attacks and strokes by dissolving blood clots. The market is categorized into fibrin-specific and non-fibrin-specific drugs. Fibrin-specific drugs target fibrin found in clots, offering more efficient and targeted action with fewer side effects. Non-fibrin-specific drugs, on the other hand, act more broadly, impacting both fibrin and other components in the blood, which may increase the risk of bleeding and other complications.</p></p>
