<p><ul><li>LCD</li><li>LCOS</li><li>DLP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial projector market comprises three main technologies: LCD, LCoS, and DLP. LCD projectors utilize liquid crystal displays for vibrant colors and clarity, making them ideal for static presentations. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) projectors offer high resolution and superior image quality, suitable for intricate visuals and detailed images. DLP (Digital Light Processing) projectors use micro-mirrors for fast response times and reliability, often favored for motion visuals in larger venues. Each type addresses specific needs in commercial settings.</p></p>
