<p><p>ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance) measurement devices are categorized into digital and analog types. Digital meters are often favored for their accuracy and ease of use, while analog versions appeal to users who prefer traditional methods and simpler designs, impacting market dynamics accordingly.</p></p>
