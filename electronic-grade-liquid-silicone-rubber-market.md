<p><p>The Electronic Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) market is divided into two main types: Single Component LSR and Two Component LSR. Single Component LSR is pre-mixed and requires no additional components, making it user-friendly and fast-curing, ideal for applications that demand quick processing. In contrast, Two Component LSR involves combining a base with a curing agent, allowing for customized properties and enhanced performance in demanding electronic applications, such as encapsulation and potting of electronic components.</p></p>
