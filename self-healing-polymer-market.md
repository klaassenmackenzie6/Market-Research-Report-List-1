<p><ul><li>Vascular</li><li>Intrinsic</li><li>Capsule Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-healing polymer market is categorized into vascular, intrinsic, and capsule-based systems. Vascular systems use interconnected networks filled with healing agents that flow into cracks when damage occurs. Intrinsic systems rely on polymers with inherent reversible bonds or dynamic chemistry that autonomously repair damage. Capsule-based systems embed microcapsules containing healing agents that rupture upon damage, releasing the agent to mend cracks. Each type offers unique advantages in durability, efficiency, and application suitability across industries like automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
