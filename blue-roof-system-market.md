<p><strong>The Blue Roof System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Blue Roof System</li><li>Passive Blue Roof System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Blue Roof System market is categorized into two types: Active and Passive Blue Roof Systems. Active Blue Roof Systems utilize technology to manage stormwater dynamically, employing sensors and control mechanisms to optimize water retention and release. In contrast, Passive Blue Roof Systems rely on a more straightforward approach, using structural features to retain water without active monitoring, allowing for natural drainage. Both systems aim to enhance urban resilience, manage stormwater effectively, and contribute to sustainable building practices.</p></p>
