<p><p>Travel Fault Locator Systems are essential for detecting faults in electrical networks. The Single-Ended Traveling Wave Fault Locator uses a single measurement point to identify fault location by analyzing the time taken for wave signals to travel. In contrast, the Double-Ended Traveling Wave Fault Locator employs two measurement points, enhancing precision through comparative analysis of wave arrivals. Other systems may include hybrid approaches or alternative technologies, catering to specific operational needs. Each type is designed to improve fault detection and network reliability.</p></p>
