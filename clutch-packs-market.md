<p><p>Clutch packs are essential components in transmission systems, enabling smooth gear shifts by engaging and disengaging power transfer. The **Single Disc Clutch Pack** uses one friction disc and one steel plate, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for lighter-duty applications. The **Multi Disc Clutch Pack** incorporates multiple alternating friction discs and steel plates, providing higher torque capacity and durability, ideal for heavy-duty or high-performance vehicles. Both types cater to varying demands, balancing efficiency, performance, and application-specific requirements in automotive and industrial markets.</p></p>
