<p><p>The Pedestrian AEB (Autonomous Emergency Braking) System market includes Forward Collision Warning (FCW), Dynamic Brake Support (DBS), and Crash Imminent Braking (CIB). FCW alerts drivers of potential collisions with pedestrians. DBS assists by amplifying braking force when the driver reacts insufficiently. CIB automatically applies brakes to prevent or mitigate collisions if the driver fails to respond. These systems enhance pedestrian safety by detecting, warning, and intervening to avoid accidents, reducing injury severity and improving road safety.</p></p>
