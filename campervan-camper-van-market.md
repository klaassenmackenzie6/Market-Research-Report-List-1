<p><p>The campervan market is primarily divided into two types: Fixed Roof (FR) campervans and Rising Roof (RR) campervans. Fixed Roof campervans feature a permanent roof design, providing consistent height and more space for storage, making them suitable for year-round use. In contrast, Rising Roof campervans have a pop-up roof mechanism, which allows for increased headroom and ventilation while parked, enhancing living space. Both designs cater to different preferences in terms of comfort, convenience, and mobility for travelers.</p></p>
