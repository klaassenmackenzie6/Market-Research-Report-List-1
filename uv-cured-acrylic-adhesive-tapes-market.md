<p><p>The UV-cured acrylic adhesive tapes market is segmented into foamed and non-foamed tapes, catering to different application needs. Foamed tapes feature a cushioning layer that provides enhanced conformability and impact resistance, making them ideal for uneven surfaces and demanding environments. Non-foamed tapes, on the other hand, offer a thinner profile with strong adhesion, suitable for applications requiring precise bonding without added thickness. Both types leverage UV-curing technology for rapid curing and superior performance in various industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
