<p><ul><li>Hammer Forging Die</li><li>Hot Extrusion Die</li><li>Die Casting Die</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die steel market encompasses various types tailored for specific manufacturing processes. Hammer forging dies facilitate the shaping of metal by rapidly delivering impacts, while hot extrusion dies are used to create complex shapes by forcing heated metal through a die. Die casting dies, on the other hand, mold molten metal into precise forms under high pressure. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing the selection of materials and design based on the required durability, heat resistance, and machining precision.</p></p>
