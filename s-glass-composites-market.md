<p><ul><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Pultrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The S-Glass composites market is driven by various manufacturing processes, including filament winding, injection molding, and pultrusion. Filament winding involves winding continuous fibers around a mandrel to create lightweight structures, suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. Injection molding allows for the creation of complex shapes by injecting resin into molds, ideal for mass production of components. Pultrusion, a continuous process, combines fibers and resin to create long, strong profiles, widely used in construction and infrastructure sectors. Each method serves distinct applications within the composite market.</p></p>
