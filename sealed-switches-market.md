<p><p>The sealed switches market is segmented into various types, including SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw), SPST-NC (Single Pole Single Throw - Normally Closed), and SPST-NO (Single Pole Single Throw - Normally Open). SPDT switches allow connection to one of two outputs, offering versatility in circuit design. SPST-NC switches are closed in their default state and open when activated, ideal for fail-safe applications. Conversely, SPST-NO switches remain open until activated, commonly used in control circuits. Each type serves distinct functionalities in various applications.</p></p>
