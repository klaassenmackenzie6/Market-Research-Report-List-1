<p><p>The acoustic-insulation rock wool market is segmented into four main types: felty, strip, tubular, and granular. Felty rock wool is designed for sound absorption and is often used in walls and ceilings. Strip rock wool, flexible and easy to handle, is commonly used for insulating gaps. Tubular rock wool is specifically shaped for pipes and ducts, providing thermal and acoustic insulation. Granular rock wool is utilized in loose-fill applications, effective for filling cavities and enhancing soundproofing in various structures.</p></p>
