<p><p>The acute blood purification devices market comprises three main types: Continuous Blood Purification (CBP), Hemodialysis (HD), and Other market devices. CBP is designed for continuous treatment of critical patients, ensuring stable removal of toxins and fluids over an extended period. Hemodialysis, on the other hand, is a more periodic treatment that filters blood through a dialyzer to remove waste. Other devices may include therapeutic apheresis and plasma exchange, tailored for various clinical needs and conditions.</p></p>
