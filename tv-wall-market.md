<p><p>The TV wall market encompasses several display technologies. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) utilizes backlighting for vibrant images but is relatively thin. LED (Light Emitting Diode) is a subtype of LCD that offers better energy efficiency and brightness through individual light sources. Rear Projection (DLP - Digital Light Processing) employs micro-mirrors for high-quality images in larger formats, suitable for big-screen applications. PDP (Plasma Display Panel) provides excellent color accuracy and contrast, ideal for immersive viewing experiences, though it is less common today.</p></p>
