<p><p>The Night Vision Riflescope market primarily includes two main types: thermal imaging scopes and other night vision technologies. Thermal imaging scopes detect heat emitted by objects, allowing users to see in complete darkness and through obstructions like fog or smoke. These are particularly useful for hunting and surveillance. "Other" night vision technologies encompass devices that amplify available light, such as image intensification scopes, which enhance visibility under low-light conditions. Both types cater to various applications, including military, law enforcement, and civilian use.</p></p>
