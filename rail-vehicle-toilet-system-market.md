<p><ul><li>Vacuum Toilet System</li><li>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail vehicle toilet system market primarily comprises two types: vacuum toilet systems and pressured water flushing toilet systems. Vacuum toilet systems utilize a low-water, vacuum-assisted mechanism that efficiently removes waste, minimizing water use and reducing odors. In contrast, pressured water flushing toilet systems rely on pressurized water to flush waste, ensuring cleanliness but consuming more water. Both systems are designed for efficiency and hygiene in rail environments, catering to varying passenger and operational needs.</p></p>
