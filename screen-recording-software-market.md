<p><p>The screen recording software market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software offers users the convenience of accessing and storing recordings online, facilitating collaboration and remote access. This type often features subscription models and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed directly on a user’s device or organization’s server, providing greater control over data security and performance. Users typically prefer on-premises options for sensitive information or when consistent internet access is an issue.</p></p>
