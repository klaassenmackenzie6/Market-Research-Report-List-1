<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Suppressors market primarily includes three types: diode arrays, polymer ESD suppressors, and Transient Voltage Suppressors (TVS). Diode arrays consist of multiple diodes configured to clamp voltage spikes, providing robust protection. Polymer ESD suppressors utilize conductive polymers to manage voltage surges and are known for their rapid response times. TVS devices protect sensitive electronics by diverting excess voltage to ground, effectively safeguarding circuits from ESD events and ensuring the reliability of electronic components.</p></p>
