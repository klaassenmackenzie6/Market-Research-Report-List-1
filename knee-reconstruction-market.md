<p><p>The knee reconstruction market includes several implant types: </p><p>1. **Cemented Implants** use bone cement for secure fixation, ideal for older patients with reduced bone quality. </p><p>2. **Cementless Implants** promote natural bone growth into the implant, suitable for younger, active patients. </p><p>3. **Partial Implants** focus on replacing only damaged knee components, preserving healthy tissue for less invasive procedures. </p><p>4. **Revision Implants** address failures or complications from previous surgeries, ensuring stability and function in challenging cases. Each type caters to specific patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
