<p><p>The medical diagnostic software market is divided into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed locally on healthcare facilities' servers, offering greater control over data and security but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing easier access, scalability, and cost-effectiveness through subscription models. However, it may raise concerns about data privacy and reliance on internet connectivity, making each option suitable for different healthcare needs.</p></p>
