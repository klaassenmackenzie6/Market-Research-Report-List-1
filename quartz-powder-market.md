<p><p>The quartz powder market is primarily categorized into press molding and casting molding types. Press molding involves forming materials by applying pressure to compact the quartz powder, resulting in high-density components suitable for various applications. In contrast, casting molding involves pouring a liquid mixture of quartz powder into molds, allowing it to solidify into desired shapes. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including ceramics, electronics, and construction, with each type offering unique advantages in terms of product characteristics and production efficiency.</p></p>
