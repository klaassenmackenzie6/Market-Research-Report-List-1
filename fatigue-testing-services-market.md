<p><ul><li>Low Cycle Fatigue (LCF)</li><li>High Cycle Fatigue (HCF)</li><li>Thermomechanical Fatigue (TF)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fatigue testing services market is categorized into several types: Low Cycle Fatigue (LCF), High Cycle Fatigue (HCF), and Thermomechanical Fatigue (TF). LCF examines material behavior under large strain cycles, typically seen in applications like structural components. HCF focuses on materials subjected to many cycles of lower stress, common in rotating machinery. Thermomechanical Fatigue (TF) assesses the combined effects of thermal and mechanical stresses, crucial in high-temperature environments. Other testing types may include specific methodologies tailored for unique material or industry requirements.</p></p>
