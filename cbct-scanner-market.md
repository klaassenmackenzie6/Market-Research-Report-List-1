<p><p>The CBCT scanner market consists of several key types, including dental X-ray scanners, panoramic X-ray systems, and cephalometric X-ray systems. Panoramic X-ray systems provide a wide-angle view of the entire dental arch, aiding in diagnosis and treatment planning.
