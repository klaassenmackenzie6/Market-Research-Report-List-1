<p><p>The industrial sweeper market is categorized into two primary types: walk-behind sweepers and rider-on sweepers. Walk-behind sweepers are manually operated, compact machines designed for smaller spaces, allowing operators to navigate tight areas effectively. In contrast, rider-on sweepers are designed for larger industrial environments, enabling operators to sit while using the machine, enhancing productivity and reducing fatigue. Both types serve the essential function of maintaining clean surfaces in warehouses, factories, and outdoor areas, improving safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
