<p><p>The air scrubber market consists of two primary types: wet air scrubbers and dry air scrubbers. Wet air scrubbers utilize liquid solutions, typically water, to capture and remove pollutants from the air, effectively addressing gases and particulate matter. In contrast, dry air scrubbers use solid adsorbents or chemical reagents to remove contaminants without the use of water. Both types play crucial roles in industrial settings, improving air quality and complying with environmental regulations while addressing specific emission control needs.</p></p>
