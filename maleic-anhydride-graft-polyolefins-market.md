<p><p>Maleic Anhydride Graft Polyolefins (MAH-g-PO) are specialized copolymers formed by grafting maleic anhydride onto polyolefins like polypropylene or polyethylene. These materials enhance compatibility between hydrophobic and hydrophilic phases, making them valuable in applications such as coatings, adhesives, and composites. The unique properties of MAH-g-POs, including improved adhesion, thermal stability, and chemical resistance, are driving their adoption in various industries, including automotive, packaging, and construction.</p><p>The Maleic Anhydride Graft Polyolefins Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
