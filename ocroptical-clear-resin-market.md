<p><p>Optical Clear Resin (OCR) is widely used in various applications for its clarity and durability. The market is primarily divided into two types: Silicone-based and Acrylic-based. Silicone-based OCRs are known for their flexibility, thermal stability, and resistance to yellowing, making them ideal for applications requiring high durability and long-lasting performance. In contrast, Acrylic-based OCRs offer excellent optical clarity, easier processing, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for applications where aesthetics and budget are key considerations. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing product performance and longevity.</p></p>
