<p><ul><li>Screw Extruder</li><li>Forming Machine</li><li>Vulcanizing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire building machinery market includes several key types: Screw Extruders, Forming Machines, and Vulcanizing Machines. Screw Extruders are used for mixing and shaping rubber materials into tire components. Forming Machines then assemble these components into the desired tire shape, ensuring precision and consistency. Finally, Vulcanizing Machines apply heat and pressure to cure the rubber, enhancing its durability and performance. Together, these technologies facilitate the efficient production of high-quality tires, catering to the automotive industry's demands.</p></p>
