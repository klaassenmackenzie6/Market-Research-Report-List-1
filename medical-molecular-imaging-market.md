<p><p>The medical molecular imaging market encompasses several key imaging technologies. MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) provides detailed images of soft tissues using magnetic fields and radio waves. CT (Computed Tomography) combines X-ray measurements to create cross-sectional images for accurate diagnostics. PET (Positron Emission Tomography) detects metabolic processes using radioactive tracers, while SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography) visualizes blood flow and activity by detecting gamma rays. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves to generate real-time images, particularly for examining soft tissues and organs. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
