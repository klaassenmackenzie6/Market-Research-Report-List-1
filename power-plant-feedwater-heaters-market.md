<p><p>The Power Plant Feedwater Heaters market includes two primary types: low-pressure and high-pressure feedwater heaters. Low-pressure feedwater heaters operate at lower temperature and pressure conditions, efficiently utilizing steam from the extraction of turbines to preheat water, enhancing overall thermal efficiency. In contrast, high-pressure feedwater heaters operate at elevated temperatures and pressures, designed to optimize energy recovery in more demanding conditions. Both types are critical for improving boiler efficiency and reducing fuel consumption in power generation, contributing to improved economic and environmental performance.</p></p>
