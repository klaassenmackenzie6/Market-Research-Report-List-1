<p><p>The aerospace electrically conductive sealants market is segmented into three main types: nickel-filled, silver-filled, and copper-filled sealants. Nickel-filled sealants provide good conductivity and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for various aerospace applications. Silver-filled sealants offer superior electrical conductivity and thermal performance, ideal for high-demand environments. Copper-filled sealants are cost-effective and provide reliable conductivity; however, they may have limitations in corrosion resistance. These sealants are crucial for ensuring effective electromagnetic shielding and moisture protection in aerospace components.</p></p>
