Whey-based replacers are derived from whey protein, providing essential nutrients and amino acids for young animals, promoting growth and health. Skim-based replacers, on the other hand, utilize skim milk powder, delivering a closer nutritional profile to natural milk. Both types cater to different feeding requirements in livestock, dairy calves, and other young animals, enhancing their development and reducing dependency on maternal milk.</p></p>
