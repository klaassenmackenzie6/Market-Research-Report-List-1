<p><p>The conveyors market comprises various types catering to specific material handling needs. Roller conveyors facilitate the movement of goods using cylindrical rollers, ideal for heavy items. Belt conveyors use continuous belts to transport materials smoothly over distances. Overhead conveyors utilize overhead tracks, saving floor space while enabling efficient product movement. Pallet conveyors are designed for the transport of palletized loads, providing stable handling. Other market segments include specialized conveyors like screw, chain, and magnetic conveyors, tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
