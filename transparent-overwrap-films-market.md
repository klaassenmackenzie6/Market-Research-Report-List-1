<p><p>The transparent overwrap films market primarily consists of shrink films and stretch films, each serving distinct packaging needs. Shrink films are used to tightly encase products by applying heat, leading to a secure fit that offers protection and tamper evidence. Conversely, stretch films are designed to extend and wrap around items, providing stability and support during transport without heat application. Both types enhance product visibility and security, making them essential in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
